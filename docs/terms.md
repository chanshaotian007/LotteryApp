# Terms of Service Draft

LotteryApp provides random and statistical lottery-number generation tools. It does not provide lottery sales, ticket purchasing, ticket issuance, purchase agency, prize claims, or betting payment services.

Generated numbers, model scores, and backtest reports are informational only. Lottery outcomes are random, and no feature guarantees a prize or improved odds. Users are responsible for checking official rules and announcements.

Premium subscriptions unlock software features such as history exclusion, model reports, budget generation, and advanced statistics. Subscription fees are not lottery stakes and do not purchase lottery tickets.
