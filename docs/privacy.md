# Privacy Policy Draft

LotteryApp collects only the data needed to provide app functionality:

- Generated number requests, selected game type, generation mode, budget bounds, and random seed when sent to the server.
- Membership entitlement status returned by the billing platform after purchase verification.
- Operational logs needed for abuse prevention, debugging, and rate limiting.

The service does not require real-name betting data, lottery ticket photos, or payment information for ticket purchase because it is not a lottery sales platform. Subscription billing should be processed by Google Play Billing, and the server should store only the minimum purchase-token validation result needed to grant entitlement.
